Everything the club has read, in order.
Nomination week (1 week)
  • One nomination per member, and it has to be a book you haven't read yourself. Honor system — we don't check.
  • New nominations stay hidden while nomination week is open and reveal together once voting opens. Rollover books from last cycle are already public, so they show up right away.
  • No one is required to nominate.
  • If the same book gets nominated by two or more people independently, it merges into one entry with a head start: 2 points for a double-nomination, 3 for a triple (capped at 3). Both pitches are shown side by side.
If nothing gets nominated
  • If a cycle ends with zero new nominations and zero rollovers, Mor'gathuul the Atrocious, God of Vorlathiss, claims the club's souls and we are disbanded for all eternity. (Not really — nomination week just automatically extends.)
Voting week (1 week)
  • Every member gets 3 points to distribute however they want — all 3 on one book, or split across a few.
  • Instead of points, you can use your one veto to instantly eliminate a book. Using it forfeits your 3 points that round.
  • You can't vote on a book you nominated (or co-nominated, for a merged double/triple).
  • During voting week, nothing is visible — no totals, no individual votes. Once results are in, each book's final point total is shown, and a veto reveals who cast it.
Results
  • Highest point total wins. Ties are broken first by whichever book got votes from the most unique people, then by whichever was submitted earliest.
  • Only the winning book's nominator(s) are revealed. Everyone else stays anonymous.
  • A vetoed book shows who vetoed it and what its point total would've been.
Rollover
  • The 2nd and 3rd place finishers automatically roll into next cycle's nomination list, points reset to zero. (Just 2nd place if there were only 2 nominees.)
  • A book can roll over up to twice (3 cycles total) before it has to be freshly renominated to stay eligible.
